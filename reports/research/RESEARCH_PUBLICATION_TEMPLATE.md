# Research publication template

Status date: YYYY-MM-DD

## Abstract

Summarize the research question, data, method, main result, decision, and limitation
in one short section.

## Research question

State the question the report answers.

## Hypothesis

State the tested hypothesis and the failure condition.

## Dataset

Document:

- Source artifacts
- Asset universe
- Date range, if known
- Feature set
- Data quality constraints
- Reproducibility path

## Methodology

Document the models, baselines, backtest method, costs, validation method, and
interpretation rules.

## Experimental design

Document candidate selection, grouping, metrics, controls, and decision thresholds.

## Results

Report both successful and failed outcomes.

## Statistical tests

List completed tests and tests still required.

## Failure analysis

Explain where the hypothesis failed, what broke, and what should be rejected or
excluded.

## Economic interpretation

Explain the plausible mechanism, if any. If no mechanism exists, say so.

## Limitations

State what the report does not prove.

## Decision

Choose one:

- Reject
- Defer
- Continue research
- Move to manual paper observation
- Retire

## Next experiment

Define the next evidence-producing action.
